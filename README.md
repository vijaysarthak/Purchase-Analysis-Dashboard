# 📊 Customer Purchase Analysis Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📝 Overview

This Power BI dashboard project presents a comprehensive analysis of customer purchase behavior based on product category, age group, city, and individual customer activity. The objective of this dashboard is to help stakeholders understand customer preferences, top-performing products, and spending patterns for data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📁 Dataset

* **File Name**: `Dataset.csv`
* **Records**: 1,578 customer purchases
* **Key Columns**:

  * `PurchaseDate`
  * `ProductCategory`
  * `CustomerName`
  * `PurchaseAmount`
  * `City`
  * `AgeGroup`

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* **Power BI**: For interactive data visualization and dashboard creation
* **Microsoft Excel & PostgreSQL**: For initial data cleaning

## 🔍 Insights Summary

* **Home Decor** is the top-performing category in both volume and value.
* Customers aged **50+** contribute nearly **50%** of the revenue.
* **Jane Smith** is the highest-value customer in terms of purchase frequency and total value.
* **New York** has the highest spending customers on average.
* Strong potential for personalized marketing towards the **50+ age group** and **high-value customers** like Jane Smith and John Doe.
